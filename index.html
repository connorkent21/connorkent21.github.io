About

Get to know Connor Kent

I have always had a love for programming since I wrote my first chunky HTML webpage at 15. Since then I’ve been hooked; spending my spare time making fun (at least to me) little programs to expand my knowledge on different aspects of programming. In addition to my school-required, backend/algorithm heavy programs with C++, I’ve spent time delving into RESTful API’s and JSON; making programs connecting to the ISS, translation APIs, stock and crypto data, and even writing my own program to write and access my own JSON data. Since then I’ve been fascinated with the idea of putting a face to all the code I’ve been writing behind the scenes.
